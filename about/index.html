Jon Heslop is a web designer working at Rapha, previously he worked with the people behind Hiut Denim Co., The Do Book Co. and The Do Lectures. He spends his weekends roaming the city with friends, riding his bike and doing DIY botch jobs.

Jon interacts with others using Twitter, keeps a Tumblr, GimmeBar and Very Goods of interesting things he finds. He makes a habit of listening to music all the time. He loves sitting back and catching up on his instapaper or reading a good book.

Jon is 25 years old and is currently living in London. He began life in London, grew up on the Wirral and later the Midlands. You can email him if you like.
